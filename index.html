About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Scientific Computing, Modeling & Simulation (SCMS), SPPU, Pune. My research focuses on studying biological systems at the molecular scale — the role of proteins involved in cell signalling, drug delivery, and drug design. My expertise includes first-principles calculations of materials, molecular dynamics simulations of biological systems, and data processing & analysis. I am looking forward to opportunities in the pharmaceutical industry, data science, and materials science labs.

Research

My work covers:

  • Theoretical investigations and first-principles calculations of materials (including potential spintronic and thermoelectric materials).
  • Molecular Dynamics simulation studies (e.g., interaction of Ru-complex with calf thymus DNA).
  • Interaction study of collagen with the α−2A domain of Integrin.
